🌴 My Favorite Beaches

Cocoa Beach (Florida): Chill surf town with laid-back vibes, soft sand, and perfect beginner waves 🏄‍♀️🌅

Clearwater Beach (Florida): Sparkling white sand + turquoise water = the ultimate beach day. Sunset pier is a must 🌞🎡

Jupiter Beach (Florida): Serene, scenic, and a local fave for long walks and shell-hunting. Bonus: Sea turtles! 🐢💙

Laguna Beach (California): Coves, cliffs, and a postcard-worthy view every step of the way 🎨🌴

Outer Banks (North Carolina): Windy dunes, lighthouse views, and major summer nostalgia energy 💨🌊🏖️
